EyeWriter

Developed in collaboration with Zach's working group thesystemis, Eye writer is the combination eye tracking software and a software program to enable those with ALS and others to communicate and create.

"Art is a tool of empowerment and social change, and I consider myself blessed to be able to create and use my work to promote health reform, bring awareness about ALS and help others"

Tempt One, artist

Reface

Reface [Portrait Sequencer] (2007: Golan Levin and Zachary Lieberman) is a surreal video mash-up that composes endless combinations of its visitors' faces. Based on the Victorian "Exquisite Corpse" parlor game, the Reface installation records and dynamically remixes brief video slices of its viewers' mouths, eyes and brows.
